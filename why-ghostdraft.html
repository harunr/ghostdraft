Building Stronger Customer Connections

GhostDraft optimizes the customer communications management process through innovative document automation capabilities, enabling insurers to easily craft an engaging experience through improved communications.

Get Started

Digitally native

We are a modern, next-gen CCM solution. The GhostDraft platform was built from the ground-up to provide a full-spectrum, digital experience – much more than just a document print engine. Our loosely coupled service-oriented architecture (SOA) enables flexible, scalable and rapid integration, with a light implementation footprint.

Learn more

Business-Driven Solutions

Our technology has been deployed as a foundational component of applications in the Insurance, Banking, Property and Legal sectors but our focus today is primarily on North American insurance market, particularly Property & Casualty. Our customer-services implementation team have acquired significant ‘in market’ domain experience with complex insurance products.

Learn more

Global Delivery Model

Our US and Canadian team can deliver training and implementation support on-site, with optional ‘follow the sun’ services that leverage our offshore Centre of Excellence in South Africa. This delivery model, along with our Agile approach to projects, serves to reduce inefficiencies and cut time to production, allowing us to pass along significant cost savings to our customers.

Learn more

Integrated with the Best

Our powerful allies include some of the industry’s leading core-system and policy administration vendors. For many of these, GhostDraft is an integral part of their offering and functions as their primary customer communications system. Our unique data domain model has enabled fully-modular, flexible integration with these systems.

Learn more

Future-Proof Technology

Constantly asking ‘what’s next’ for our customers and the market. Expect to see: a modernized design-studio UI; an investment in enhanced workflow tools that facilitate collaboration between internal and external participants in the forms product-management lifecycle; along with run-time insertion and editing of extraneous documents, such as manuscript endorsement.
